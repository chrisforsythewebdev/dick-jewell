The Queen's Speech With H's Dropped (2005)

DVD – 8min 44sec / Dick Jewell

The Queen’s Speech With H’s Dropped is an attempt to render the ‘Queen’s English’, as spoken by the Queen, more familiar to the local colloquial English of the capital.
The outcome strengthens the absurdities of the accent and dialogue of subjects of the realm when addressing members of the royal family.
