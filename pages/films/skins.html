SKINS (1979)

Super 8 / 8min 46sec / Dick Jewell

Skins (extract)

'SKINS' has exhibited at East End Promise, A Story of Cultural Migrants, 9–24 Oct 2010, LONDONEWCASTLE Project Space, London E2.

SKINS
Documentary shot in 1979 on Super 8 during the second wave of 'skins' in England.
It's about skinheads meeting up and hanging out at The Last Resort in Goulston St E1,
where they could get cropped, booted and suited. The soundtrack harks back
to the original 'skins' that had morphed from the 'mods' about ten years before.
