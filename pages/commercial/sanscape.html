Kalahari Warriors (Squiddly Mix): Animal Radio / Dick Jewell 1996

Kalahari Warriors
I produced this video on a zero budget as all proceeds from the CD it is promoting go directly to support the Bushmen (Koisan) of the Kalahari.
A mixture of Hi8 footage that I shot of the Koisan in the Korridors of Namibia, on the border with Botswana, at the time of the original recording of Ngube Gout playing the track on his thumb piano, and footage that I'd shot previously towards making a TV programme called 'Men Behaving Sadly' in Tenerife.
