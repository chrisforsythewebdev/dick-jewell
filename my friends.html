My Friends on my Kinky Gerlinky MySpace November 2007 - April 2008
inkjet prints, tamperproof noticeboard 90 x 129 cm

In this piece the notion of online acquaintances by way of Myspace friends become a physical reality,
whilst also charting the development of the contemporary self-portrait
from the notion of a passport approved image into the personalised digital portrait.
The public aspect of collecting friends on the Internet is maintained by re-displaying the portraits on a public notice board.
